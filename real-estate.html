Sarajevo

Heart of Bosnia

Things To See: Baščaršija (Old Bazaar), Gazi Husrev-beg Mosque, Trebević Cable Car, Vrelo Bosne, Sarajevo City Center Shopping Mall

Places To Stay (Recommended): Hotel Europe, Swissôtel Sarajevo, Hotel Hills, boutique hotels in the Old Town.

Travnik

The Vizier's Town and Blue Water

Things To See: Travnik Fortress, Painted Mosque, Sulejmanija Mosque, Plava voda (Blue Water spring), Travnik Kebab

Places To Stay (Recommended): Hotel Lipa

Number of Days: 1

Jajce

Waterfalls

Places To Stay (Recommended): Hotels in Jajce with lake views, apartments or guesthouses offering a closer connection to nature.

Number of Days: 1 - 2 days
