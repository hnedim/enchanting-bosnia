Sarajevo

The Heart of Bosnia, Where East Meets West

Discover Sarajevo

Sarajevo, the vibrant capital of Bosnia and Herzegovina, is a city where centuries of history intertwine with modern life. From the Ottoman charm of Baščaršija to Austro-Hungarian architecture, and surrounded by majestic mountains, Sarajevo offers a unique blend of cultures, cuisines, and captivating stories. It's a city that has risen from adversity, now bustling with cafes, artisan shops, and a resilient spirit.

Places to Visit in Sarajevo

Baščaršija (Old Bazaar)

Step back in time in Sarajevo's charming Ottoman-era bazaar. Explore artisan shops, enjoy traditional Bosnian coffee, and sample local delicacies like ćevapi. Don't miss the Sebilj fountain.

  • Sebilj Fountain
  • Copper Street
  • Gazi Husrev-beg Mosque

Vrelo Bosne (Springs of River Bosna)

A serene natural park at the foot of Mount Igman, known for its picturesque walking paths, crystal-clear springs, and lush greenery. Perfect for a relaxing escape from the city.

  • Nature Walks
  • Natural Springs
  • Horse-drawn Carriages

Bjelašnica and Igman Mountains

Host of the 1984 Winter Olympics, these mountains offer stunning scenery, hiking trails, and skiing opportunities. Ideal for adventure seekers and nature lovers, accessible year-round.

  • Skiing & Snowboarding (winter)
  • Hiking & Mountain Biking (summer)
  • Olympic Sites

Sarajevo Tunnel of Hope

A profound historical site, this underground tunnel was vital during the siege of Sarajevo. It offers a poignant look into the city's recent past and the resilience of its people.

  • Historical Significance
  • Museum Exhibits
  • Symbol of Resilience

Latin Bridge & Emperor's Mosque

Explore the iconic Latin Bridge, near the spot where Archduke Franz Ferdinand was assassinated, sparking WWI. Nearby, the grand Emperor's Mosque stands as a testament to Ottoman architecture.

  • WWI History
  • Ottoman Architecture
  • Riverside Walks

Where to Stay in Sarajevo

Sarajevo offers a range of accommodation options to suit every preference, from luxury hotels to cozy boutique stays and authentic traditional guesthouses. Enchanting Bosnia can help you find the perfect place.

Old Town (Baščaršija) Hotels

Stay in the heart of history, surrounded by charming streets, artisan shops, and traditional restaurants. Perfect for immersing yourself in the local culture.

City Center & Marijin Dvor

For a more modern experience with easy access to shopping malls, business centers, and contemporary cafes. Offers a blend of history and urban convenience.

Ilidža Area Resorts

Ideal for those seeking tranquility and greenery, especially near Vrelo Bosne. Features spa resorts and family-friendly accommodations, often with beautiful gardens.

Mountain Chalets & Hotels

For nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts, especially during winter for skiing or summer for hiking. Offers stunning views and a peaceful retreat.

Recommended Time to Spend

For a comprehensive experience of Sarajevo's main attractions, history, and local atmosphere, we recommend spending **3 to 5 days** in the city. This allows ample time to:

  • Explore Baščaršija thoroughly.
  • Visit key museums (e.g., Tunnel of Hope, National Museum).
  • Take a half-day trip to Vrelo Bosne.
  • Enjoy the local cuisine and coffee culture.
  • Possibly a day trip to the surrounding mountains (Bjelašnica/Igman).

For longer stays or specific interests, we can craft a custom itinerary for you!
